Adoption Fees

Adoption fees include vet checks, vaccination, flea and worm treatment.

The fees are as follows:

  • Cats $130
  • Dogs $250
  • Rabbits $130
  • Guinea Pigs $10
  • Birds $10
  • Kittens $100
  • Mice $10
  • Puppies $200
  • Fish $5

If you wish to give an additional donation over and above the adoption fee, this would be greatly appreciated.
