Caring for Cats

You will need some basic supplies before bringing your new cat home.

    Cat checklist

  • Cat carrier box
  • Food and water bowls
  • Food
  • Litter tray and litter
  • Scratching post
  • Toys
  • Safety collar
  • Soft bed

Set up one room


  • Keep your cat in one room for 2-3 days. A quiet room is ideal.
  • Ensure the room is secure and well-ventilated.
  • Set up the room with water, food, toys, litter tray, scratching post and somewhere to hide.
  • Provide a bed or comfy blanket to help your cat settle.
  • Remove dangerous wires, curtain cords, or items your cat can chew or get tangled in.
  • Remove breakable or scratchable items
  • After 2-3 days in one room, slowly introduce your cat to the rest of the house.
  • Do this room by room to avoid overwhelming them.
